About Me


I'm a senior mechanical engineering student and Schulich Leader Scholar at the University of British Columbia - Okanagan Campus. I have an infectious love of learning and an overwhelming sense of curiosity and purpose!

My ultimate value in life is the pursuit of education, and I believe my purpose is to contribute to human's scientific evolution. Someday, I hope to accomplish this by contributing to a large scale space telescope, like the Hubble or James Webb -- or better yet, by becoming an astronaut!

Throughout my undergraduate degree, I have culminated experience in space science and biomedial research, engineering project management, and science journalism. I've also led several successful design teams in humanitarian engineering and space science engineering.

Feel free to explore this page to take a deeper look into my work experience, projects, or read some of the articles I've written. You can download a professional copy of my CV or contact me at the bottom of the page. Thanks for checking out my website!
